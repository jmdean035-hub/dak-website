Our Story

Dean All Knighter (DAK) began as a simple idea: bring friends together. Over the years, it has grown into a beloved tradition where people reunite, laugh, and make memories that last a lifetime.

Who Attends

While many attendees come from Arkansas, friends travel from across the U.S. to be part of DAK. It’s all about community, friendship, and good times in a relaxed tropical atmosphere.

Our Philosophy

Friendship first. Games, music, and adventures second. Whether it’s your first DAK or your tenth, you’re always welcome to join the fun.
